~ The People in Noli Me Tangere ~

By Erin Kristen F. Agbayani of 9-Emerald

Here are some characters you can find in "Noli Me Tangere" (chapters 1 to 15) by Dr. Jose Rizal.

*Note: Click on the pictures to learn more about the characters!*
#1 Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • Commonly known as Crisostomo Ibarra, is the protagonist of the story and Maria Clara’s fiance.

  • A wealthy young man of mixed Spanish and Filipino descent who studied in Europe for 7 years.

  • A well-respected gentleman in the eyes of the public, but shows strong emotions when he gets triggered by sensitive topics.
#2 Maria Clara de los Santos y Alba
  • More commonly known as Maria Clara.

  • A mestiza heroine and the fiance of Crisostomo Ibarra.

  • Seen by the people of San Diego as a timid, prim, and delicate woman who does not have much to say, but in reality has a strong personality and is very playful.

  • Her thoughts and opinions are often repressed because she is a woman who is expected to act religious, obedient, and submissive.
#3 Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Crisostomo’s father

  • The richest man in San Diego who is virtuous and generous.

  • Was falsely imprisoned because he was accused of killing a Spaniard. Afterwards, he died in prison.
#4 Don Santiago de los Santos
  • Commonly known as Kapitan Tiago.

  • Father of Maria Clara and a rich businessman in San Diego who views himself as a Spaniard and not a Filipino, even though he is a Filipino.

  • A suck-up to the Spaniards because he wanted to be like them – powerful and influential.

  • Says he is a devout Catholic even though he believes in polytheism and worships saints.
#5 Don Anastacio
  • Commonly known as Pilosopo Tasyo.

  • An intelligent man born to a rich woman who reads lots of books as a form of escapism after his wife and mother died.

  • He was dubbed as "Tasyong Baliw"; people thought of him as a lunatic because of his philosophical claims that contradicted to the religious beliefs of the people of San Diego.
